Drop Bear

In the Discworld novel The Last Continent by Terry Pratchett, Drop Bears inhabit the continent of Fourecks, a land portrayed as a parody of Australia.

  • Scientific Name: Thylarctos plummetus
  • Average Length: 130cm
  • Diet: Carnivorous
  • Habitat: Closed Canopy Forest in South Eastern Australia

Around the size of a leopard or very large dog with coarse fur and darker mottled patterning similar to Koalas. The creature is heavily built animal with powerful forearms for climbing and holding on to prey. It lacks canines, using broad powerful premolars as biting tools instead.
